About Us

Pacific Trails Resort is a haven that celebrates the beauty of California's wilderness while providing a luxurious escape for our cherished guests. Inspired by the nomadic traditions of yurts in Central Asia, we offer a sanctuary where guests can immerse themselves in nature, indulge in exciting outdoor activities.

Our Mission

At Pacific Trails Resort, our mission is to create unforgettable experiences where guests can immerse themselves in the beauty of nature, indulge in exciting outdoor activities, and bask in the comforts of luxury yurts. Through our guided hiking trips, ocean kayaking, and bird watching excursions, we aim to foster a deeper appreciation for the wonders of the natural world and inspire a sense of responsibility for preserving our environment.

Our Values

  • Respect for Nature
  • Exceptional Hospitality
  • Authentic Experiences
  • Sustainability
  • Community and Connection
